FOUNDER'S WELCOME

Welcome to Springston International School.

It is my great pleasure to welcome you to Springston International School — a place where children are nurtured not only to learn but to flourish.

My name is Mme. Doris Jennifer, and I wear several hats that are close to my heart: I am a mother, a licensed medical practitioner, a certified childcare business manager, and an educator with a passion for helping children grow into confident, compassionate individuals. Springston International School was born at the intersection of these roles — a reflection of my personal journey and professional mission.

As a mother, I understand the courage it takes to entrust your child to someone else’s care. As a nurse, I value the deep connection between a child’s health, emotional well-being, and their ability to thrive. And as an educator, I believe in creating a safe, engaging, and supportive environment where each child is encouraged to explore, express, and grow.

At Springston, we aim to do more than teach — we strive to build character, curiosity, and confidence. Our approach blends academic excellence with emotional intelligence, play-based discovery, and personalized care. Every member of our team shares a deep commitment to guiding children through these foundational years with love, professionalism, and integrity.

Whether your child is just beginning their learning journey or is already finding their path, we would be honored to walk alongside you. Thank you for considering Springston International School. And wherever your child’s path may lead, I sincerely hope it is filled with joy, discovery, and success.

Warmly,
Mme. Doris Jennifer
Founder & Director
